Food Insecurity

Whats Food Insecurity?

In a critical state of no reliable source of an efficient amount of nutritional foods. Those who experience being food insecure often develop both mental and physical health problems such as obesity, heart disease, diabetics, an increase of aggression, and anxiety; in which many could become fatal overtime.

Who is impacted?

  • 9% of the world population around 697 million people
  • 38 million people in the US are food insecure( including 12 million children)
  • On average most households that are food insecure - suffer for around seven months out of the year.

    • Whats the common cause?

  • Poverty, unemployment, low income
  • Lack of affordable housing.
  • Systemic racism and racial discrimination
  • Wars and conflicts
